Hierarchy

Cafe Río Mexican Grill

www.caferio.com

On the Cafe Río website, we can see the principle of design in the Visual Hierarchy. The prominent visual gets the attention. In this case the main dish on the menu for that day. It helps to catch the attention of people about to eat so they can sell the dish easier.

Contrast

Chile Travel

www.chile.travel

The second principle seen here is the Contrast. As we can see in this picture it is easier to know what the author wants you to see. The color, font, shape, and size take an important role to make a memorable visit to the website. It leads the user toward what is most important.

Hick's law

Viajes Falabella

www.viajesfalabella.cl

The last principle here is Hick's Law. "Hick's Law says that with every additional choice increases the time required to take a decision." We can see on this website many attractive places to spend time on vacation. So many offers that can cause the user to get undecided about what option to take.
