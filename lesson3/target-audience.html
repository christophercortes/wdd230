TARGET AUDIENCE

This website is for those expanding their businesses and bringing stuff from other countries.

PERSONAS

There are a big group of personas for this site. They are traders that bring the best quality of products from mainly China. The other personas seen here bring fruit, vegetables, and herbs.

SCENARIOS

It has been around five years since Chinese shopping malls started to operate here in Chile and they are well known for selling cheaper stuff. Locals can also start selling other products from China, such as clothes. Supermarkets are also bringing food from there. The farmer's market is also bringing food from Europe; finally, we have the people from Haiti who also sell their products here.
